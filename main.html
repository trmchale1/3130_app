Chapter 1: Infix, Prefix and Postfix Notation

Infix

  • To add A, B, we write A + B
  • To multiply, we write A * B
  • The operators go in between the operands

Prefix Notation

  • Instead of saying 'A plus B', we could say 'add A, B' and write + A B
  • For multiplication * A B

Postfix

  • Another alternative is to put the operators after the operands as in A B +

Evaluate 2+3*5

  • + First : (2 + 3) * 5 = 5 * 5 = 25
  • * First : 2 + (3 * 5) = 2 + 15 = 17
  • Infix notation requires parantheses

But Prefix Notation...

  • + 2 * 3 5 = + 2 * 3 5 = + 2 15 = 17
  • * + 2 3 5 = * 5 5 = 25
  • Does not require parantheses

Postfix

  • 2 3 5 * + = 2 15 + = 17
  • 2 3 + 5 * = 2 3 + 5 * = 5 5 * = 25
  • Does not require parantheses
